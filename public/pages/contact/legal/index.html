Frequently Asked Legal Questions

How do I request my personal data under GDPR?
Email legal@oslira.com with "GDPR Data Subject Access Request" in the subject line. Include your full name and registered email address. We'll respond within 30 days.
How can I request data deletion?
Contact our legal team at legal@oslira.com with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject. We'll process your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
Where can I find your privacy policy and terms?
Visit our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages.
