Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

1. Report First

Contact us before publicly disclosing any vulnerability

2. Provide Details

Include steps to reproduce, affected systems, and potential impact

3. Allow Time to Fix

Give us reasonable time to address the issue before disclosure (typically 90 days)

4. Act in Good Faith

Don't exploit vulnerabilities or access data beyond what's necessary to demonstrate the issue

5. Don't Cause Harm

Avoid actions that could degrade our service or destroy data
