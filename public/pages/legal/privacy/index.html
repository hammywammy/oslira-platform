Your Privacy Matters

At Oslira, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address and username
  • Encrypted password (we never store plain text passwords)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Subscription and billing information

1.2 Business Profile Data

To provide personalized AI recommendations, we collect:

  • Business name and industry information
  • Target audience descriptions
  • Value propositions and messaging preferences
  • Communication style preferences

1.3 Lead Research Data

When you use our lead analysis tools, we process:

  • Social media profile URLs you submit for analysis
  • Publicly available profile information from those URLs
  • AI-generated analysis results and scores
  • Custom notes and lead management data

1.4 Usage and Analytics Data

To improve our services, we automatically collect:

  • Platform usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Credit usage and transaction history
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • Device information and browser type

1.5 Payment Information

For billing purposes, we collect:

  • Payment method details (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Billing addresses and tax information
  • Purchase history and invoice data

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party services necessary for platform operation:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing and billing
  • Cloudflare: Security, performance, and CDN services
  • Apify: Web scraping for public data collection
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics and insights
  • Zoho Mail: Email communications

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

3.4 What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your data for advertising purposes
  • We do not access private social media content

4. Data Security and Storage

4.1 Security Measures

We implement enterprise-grade security including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • SOC 2 compliant infrastructure

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following schedule:

  • Account data: Until account deletion
  • Lead analysis data: 3 years for service improvement
  • Payment records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Usage logs: 2 years for analytics and security

5. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. You may also withdraw consent and lodge complaints with supervisory authorities.

CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have rights to know about personal information collection, delete personal information, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data), and non-discrimination for exercising rights.

5.1 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any privacy rights:

  • Email us at privacy@oslira.com
  • Use the data export/deletion tools in your account settings
  • Contact our support team through the platform

5.2 Response Timeframes

We will respond to privacy requests within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use necessary cookies for platform functionality and optional analytics cookies (with your consent) to improve our services. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Oslira is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes via email or platform notifications.
